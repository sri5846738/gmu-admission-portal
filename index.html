FAQs

Is entrance exam required?

Entrance exams are required for B.Tech, MCA, and MBA programs as per university norms.

Are scholarships available?

Yes. SSP, NSP, Merit-based, and Government scholarships are available for eligible students.

Is hostel facility available?

Yes. Separate and secure hostel facilities are available for boys and girls.

What documents are required for admission?

10th & 12th marks cards, degree marks card (for PG), Aadhaar card, transfer certificate, and passport-size photos.

Can I apply online?

Yes. You can complete the entire admission enquiry process online through this portal.

Is management quota available?

Yes. Limited management quota seats are available as per university guidelines.

Are placements provided?
